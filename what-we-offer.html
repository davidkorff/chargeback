What We Offer

Take Control Of Your Chargebacks

We are a contingency-based company. We only get paid when you profit.

The truth is, companies don’t take time to properly investigate and negotiate chargebacks. They simply write them off, believing the cost and the effort to recoup them may not be worth the reward. However, chargeback costs directly contribute to margin erosion, cutting into your profits. Also, excessive chargebacks could be a sign that there are other issues in departments across your company. Monitoring these chargebacks can help to significantly reduce the occurrence of chargebacks not only by bringing these issues to light, but showing there may be a need within the company for more communication, education, and training of cross-functional teams. You may think that chargebacks are unavoidable, and that you have to accept them. But you don’t have to to accept them.

Chargeback Recovery Logistics Inc. is a contingency-based organization dedicated to the recovery of chargebacks and deductions. We help companies collect chargebacks and decrease dilution rate, and because we work on a contingency basis, we only get paid if we succeed on your behalf. Our cross-functional team of highly trained chargeback specialists can enhance the efforts you are already making, working with your existing chargeback management department and maximizing chargeback recovery. In today’s environment, companies are becoming overwhelmed with the growing challenges of retailers’ policies. Chargeback recovery relies on multiple, cross-functional teams, including customer sales, account management, accounting, logistics and production, working in close collaboration. We see the chargeback recovery process as an “art,” which must balance polished professionalism and respect with steadfast tenacity to preserve your valuable relationships while maximizing your chargeback recovery results. We analyze your chargeback reports. With diligent and persistent efforts, we pinpoint instances where chargebacks have either been assessed in error or unnecessarily, and we negotiate with retailers on your behalf. We can also help to eliminate many future chargebacks. Remember, even if you already have a chargeback team in your organization, you will only gain when you let us help you. The experience we bring to the process can maximize your chargeback recovery rate, and at no additional cost to you.
